Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 29, 2025

Overview

This demo application (“Service”) connects a Facebook Page and Instagram account using the Meta Graph API. It is operated by Syeed Md. Jobayer solely for demonstration and evaluation purposes.

Information We Receive

When you authorize the Service, Meta shares OAuth access tokens and basic Page or Instagram Business Account details. No personal profile data is requested.

  • Page metadata (name and ID)
  • Instagram Business Account metadata (username and ID)
  • Webhook events that you have explicitly enabled in Meta

How We Use Information

The Service only uses the received data to demonstrate login state, list connected assets, and confirm webhook delivery. Data is not sold, licensed, or shared with third parties.

Data Storage & Retention

Tokens and asset metadata are kept in transient, in-memory storage while the server is running. Restarting the Service clears this data. Webhook payloads are written to application logs for troubleshooting and are automatically rotated by the host; logs are typically retained for no longer than 30 days.

Security

OAuth secrets and verify tokens are stored in environment variables on the server. HTTPS is enforced for all public endpoints, and only authorized operators have shell access to the infrastructure.

Your Choices

You can remove the integration at any time by revoking the app’s permissions from your Meta Business settings. If you need us to delete any residual log data sooner, contact us using the details below.
